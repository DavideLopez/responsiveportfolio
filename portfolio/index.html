About me

Hi! My name is Davide! Nice to meet you. I was born in 1990 and currently I live in Rome. I've always been passionate about Computers, starting at a young age when I began disassembling and configuring my first computers. As I grew up, I continued to learn more and more, eventually becoming for friends and family: "The one who solve problems!" I've held various jobs through my life, but some time ago, I began to wonder about how everything behind the internet works. Then I decided to leave my old jobs and enter the world of programming and Web Development. I attended the online Boolean Academy, an intensive 6-month course consisting of morning lessons and afternoon assignments with professional Tutors assistance. During these months of study, I learned HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, VUE.js, MySQL, PHP, and Laravel. At the end of the lessons, I obtained the certificate as a Full Stack Web Developer. On my own, I continued to study Front-End Development, which has become a source of enjoyment for me. I created this page entirely using HTML, CSS, Javascript and Framework CSS. In my free time I realize personal Portfolios for people who need one. If you're interested, click one of the buttons on top, and contact Me!
